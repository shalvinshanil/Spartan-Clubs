<p>Purposes of this organization are: 
To promote by close contact an exchange of ideas and experiences to become socially, culturally, and educationally involved with fellow students who share common interests in Pilipino/Pilipino American culture and heritage. </p>
 
<p>To establish a better understanding and appreciation of the Pilipino/Pilipino American Community and its youth as a whole. </p>
 
<p>To encourage all efforts and activities in connection with “Akbayan.”</p>
